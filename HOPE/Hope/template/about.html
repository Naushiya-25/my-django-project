About Us

“The greatness of a nation and its moral progress can be judged by the way its animals are treated.” – Mahatma Gandhi

Welcome to Animal Rescue Portal, a platform created with the goal of making it easier to report and support stray animals in need of medical assistance.

Our mission is to connect rescuers, volunteers, and donors to ensure that every animal receives the care and attention it deserves.

Why this platform matters!

Stray animals often go unnoticed or untreated due to a lack of resources or awareness. Our platform bridges the gap between people who want to help and animals that desperately need assistance.

By creating a trusted platform for reporting and fundraising, we make it easier to provide immediate care for animals in distress.

JOIN US in our mission to give a voice to those who can't speak for themselves and help create a better, kinder world for animals.
